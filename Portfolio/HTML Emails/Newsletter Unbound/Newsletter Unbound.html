Group Consultations: Delivering efficient & effective virtual or face to face healthcare
Primary care faces a huge challenge to work through the backlog created by the COVID pandemic. Group Consultations is a tried and tested approach to patient care that will help your practice to work through patient backlogs, whilst delivering better patient outcomes and building resilience in your healthcare team.

Group Consultations enable effective care for patients with long Term Conditions such as Diabetes, COPD, Chronic pain, Asthma, long COVID and more

How?
Our Group Consultations programme is flexible, giving you the training, implementation support and tools to deliver effective Group Consultations in your practice. Group Consultations are a great improvement on telephone and oneto-one video appointments and can be run either virtually or in person. Group Consultations can replace routine one-to-one appointments whilst maximising clinician time and expertise and creating a supportive peer group setting for patients. We provide the training, implementation support, resources and data to demonstrate impact, as well as collating your all-important patient feedback.
